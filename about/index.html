About me

Hello, beautiful souls! I'm Ramona, the heart and soul behind Beauty by Ramona. With a passion that blooms from the deepest roots of self-expression and wellness, I've embarked on a journey to share the transformative power of makeup, the healing essence of self-care, and the revolutionary act of self-love.

I blog because I believe in the power of sharing, in the magic of connecting with others through stories, tips, and experiences. Every post I write is an open letter to anyone who’s ever felt lost in their skin, to those who are on a quest for inner peace, and to the brave souls who are on the journey of self-discovery. Through Beauty by Ramona, I aim to inspire, to encourage, and to remind each one of you that you are beautiful, worthy, and deserving of your own love and care.

Self-care & Self-love

Self-care is the intentional practice of taking action to preserve or improve one's own health. It's about finding moments of tranquility in the chaos of everyday life, whether through a skincare routine that makes you feel rejuvenated, a quiet walk in nature that clears your mind, or a meditation session that brings you peace. It's recognizing when you need a break and giving yourself permission to pause, without guilt.

Self-love, on the other hand, is the deep-seated acceptance of who you are. It's about embracing your strengths and your flaws alike, and understanding that your worth is not defined by external achievements or the validation of others. Self-love is the foundation upon which self-care is built; it's what motivates us to nurture our well-being and pursue our happiness.
